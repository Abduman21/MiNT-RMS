Ministry of Innovation and Technology

MinT is an Ethiopian government department responsible for science and technological development in Ethiopia as well as a governing body of communications. It was established as a commission in December 1975.

Read More

History

The Ministry of Science and Technology (MoST) was established for the first time in December 1975 by proclamation No.62/1975 as a commission. It has evolved through various phases to become the current Ministry of Innovation and Technology.

Read More

Vision

To see Ethiopia entrench the capacities which enable rapid learning, adaptation and utilization of effective foreign technologies. Its mission is to coordinate and support science and technology activities for country's growth.

About MinT

Ministry of Innovation and Technology (MinT) of Ethiopia is dedicated to fostering innovation, science, and technology to accelerate the nation's development and modernization.
